Once upon a time, in a space far far away...a being not yet known banished a wizard learning to fly and a odd shamed knight...

Throughout countless eons, the wizard found distaste of those who were friendly. Flocks of sheep, cows, and pigs, villages of happy people.
Their anger from long ago, the reason they were banished. They sought the other whom followed the same sentence, only to discover more hatred...
